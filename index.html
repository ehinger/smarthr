SmartHR

Raylee Marfell

With over 25+ years’ work experience, in both Public and Private sectors, as a senior practitioner in the Human Resource field I understand the dynamics of workplace relationships and causes of conflict that can occur. I have become a trusted expert and I frequently work with groups and individuals to help them achieve successful outcomes. Please feel free contact me, if you would like to discuss a workplace challenge that is affecting you and/or others.

Over the years, what I’ve realised about myself, is that the work I’ve most enjoyed is when I’ve worked with individuals, team and organisations, to focus on the things that can make a real difference to workplace wellbeing. This includes fixing a process, problem removing obstacles, and resolving disagreements. I believe that most people come to work and want to enjoy the experience. I also know and appreciate that sometimes things aren’t always plain sailing and people need help to work through an issue or concern. I know the key to achieving positive and pragmatic solutions is to create a forum that allows respectful, safe and structured conversations. 

My portfolio includes coaching, investigations, and also organisational and people development.

Coaching – Helping people to help themselves

My approach is to explore the whole system to understand and explore cause and effect. As a coach I translate observations and work on helping to expand perspectives. I offer Individual and Team Coaching to improve Performance and Resolving Conflict. I believe that coaching offers real time learning, that is tailored to the development needs of individuals and teams. Ongoing coaching provides support for the change process to occur. Coaching conversations challenge and stretch for growth.

Investigations – Getting below the surface

I undertake independent workplace investigations for employers providing robust reliable findings and reports- without delay. I believe we should use the right intervention, to gain maximum impact with minimal harm. To achieve this, we need to move away from adversarial to constructive dialogue and balance humanity with pragmatics I am a graduate of the Association of Workplace Investigator's Training Institute http://www.awi.org/ In this space I offer the following;

  • Pre investigation - Unpack
  • Fact finding
  • Unpack what’s actually going on
  • Exploring other remedies
  • Investigate
  • Prompt, Impartial and Through
  • Post investigation - Rebuild
  • Helping individuals and teams to heal
